Standard 2-Year Warranty

1. Manufacturing Excellence:
Every Dolce & Gabbana creation is a testament to Italian tradition. We provide a limited warranty against manufacturing defects for a period of two (2) years from the date of purchase. This warranty covers structural integrity and artisanal execution.

2. Limitations and Care:
As our products often feature delicate handmade embellishments, the following are excluded from warranty coverage:

  • Artisanal Wear: The natural loss of sequins, beads, or embroidery due to friction; the fading of prints on delicate fabrics; and the characteristic marks on exotic leathers.
  • Inappropriate Care: Damage from exposure to humidity, heat, or non-specialized cleaning processes. Highly decorative items should only be handled by luxury specialists.
  • External Impacts: Accidental tearing, scratches to metal hardware (buckles/logos), and damage caused by pets or corrosive liquids.
  • Unauthorized Modification: Any intervention, repair, or resizing performed by third parties not authorized by Dolce & Gabbana.

3. Claims and Verification:
To initiate a claim, the "Certificate of Authenticity" or original receipt must be presented. Products will be inspected by our Italian quality labs. Verified defects will be resolved through repair or replacement. Refunds are restricted to legal consumer rights periods.
