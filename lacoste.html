Limited 3-Year Warranty Policy

1. Coverage Scope:
This warranty covers solely manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship for a period of three (3) years from the date of purchase. It does not cover the natural lifespan of the product.

2. Strict Exclusions (Not Covered):
To minimize validity risks, the following are strictly excluded from coverage:

  • Wear and Tear: Natural fading of color, pilling of fabric, thinning of soles, creasing of leather, or shape distortion due to regular use.
  • Improper Care: Damage caused by failure to follow the specific washing/cleaning instructions on the internal label (e.g., shrinkage, color bleeding, bleach damage).
  • Physical Damage: Tears, burns, scratches, accidental stains, or damage caused by sharp objects or external friction.
  • Alterations: Any product that has been modified, tailored, or repaired by a third party.

3. Claim Process:
Warranty claims are subject to a mandatory technical inspection. The company reserves the sole right and discretion to determine if the issue is a manufacturing defect or the result of usage. If the claim is approved, the remedy is limited to repair or replacement of the item. Refunds are not permitted under this warranty policy after the statutory return period.
